Velvet - a definition
 

Velvet is an Events Management and Marketing based business that understands what it means to be a client and the issues faced by
businesses of today, including maximizing strategic direction and brand objectives whilst meeting accountability requirements.

Velvet offers realistic and well managed project management of any event or marketing campaign, focusing on the strategy, organisation,
implementation and analysis.

With international and national experience in both event management and marketing, Velvet offers a wealth of understanding in the needs
and perspective of small to large businesses and with this knowledge can create successful and memorable events and marketing
campaigns.
